# Katchimera Image Style Guide

## Recommended Default Style

Use:

- `cozy-premium stylized 3D creature CGI`

Do not use:

- fully realistic animal rendering
- bright toy-commercial Clash Royale styling
- exaggerated Fortnite hero proportions
- heavy super-deformed chibi proportions

## Why This Style Fits

KatchaDeck is calm, reflective, and slightly magical. The creatures should feel emotionally adoptable, collectible, and premium, but not loud, childish, or overly gamey.

Best fit:

- cozy-premium 3D collectible feel
- believable materials and lighting
- stylized anatomy with slightly softened proportions
- soft cinematic realism
- warmer emotional readability
- readable silhouettes at card size

This should feel closer to:

- premium spirit companion creature art
- high-end figurine / statue render
- soft cinematic fantasy CGI with cozy warmth

It should feel less like:

- mobile battle game splash art
- toy-box comedy fantasy
- hyper-real monster concept art

## Visual Rules

- one creature only
- centered or slightly three-quarter pose
- readable silhouette
- premium moody background
- emotionally readable face
- no text
- no UI frame baked into image
- no cluttered scenery
- no multiple characters

## Material Direction

- fur, moss, bark, stone, shell, velvet, ember glow, mist, polished horn, plush fiber
- subtle subsurface glow where needed
- soft rim lighting
- premium specular highlights, not plastic toy shine

## Shape Language

- elegant silhouette first
- slightly rounded body language
- softened face and eye readability
- one or two signature details only
- avoid over-accessorizing
- avoid armor overload
- creature must still read clearly as one family across habitats and stages
- do not go fully chibi

## Stage Direction

- `sprout`: simpler, smaller, cleaner, more approachable, slightly cuter
- `shaped`: richer secondary forms, stronger markings
- `ascended`: fuller aura, stronger habitat integration, more majestic silhouette without becoming harsh

## Habitat Direction

- `home`: warmer, softer, sanctuary-lit
- `cozy`: amber, plush, intimate
- `urban`: sleek, signal-lit, reflective, but still companion-like
- `nature`: mossy, wind-touched, rooted
- `waterside`: airy, rippled, horizon-lit

## Prompt Pattern

Recommended pattern:

1. family identity
2. stage identity
3. habitat modifier
4. signature visual traits
5. material and lighting direction
6. camera / composition
7. quality / rendering style
8. negatives

## Negatives

- no text
- no watermark
- no frame
- no extra creatures
- no human
- no weapon
- no UI overlay
- no heavy chibi proportions
- no aggressive monster face
- no plastic toy finish

## Final Recommendation

Default prompt style:

- `cozy-premium stylized 3D CGI fantasy creature companion`
- `soft cinematic lighting`
- `premium spirit companion creature art`
- `calm magical mood`
- `gentle emotional readability`
- `dark atmospheric background`
